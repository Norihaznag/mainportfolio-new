## 4. Content Strategy — What to Post

### The 4 content pillars (rotate between these):

### Pillar 1: BUILD IN PUBLIC (40% of posts)
Show your work in progress. People love watching things get built.

**Examples:**
- "Just shipped a SaaS dashboard for a fintech startup. Here's what the auth flow looks like →" [screenshot]
- "Day 3 of building an MVP for [client]. The admin panel is coming together →" [video/screenshot]
- "Before vs After: Redesigned this startup's landing page. Conversion went from 1.2% to 4.8%"
- Screen recordings of you coding (30-60 sec, sped up)
- "Here's how I structure a Next.js project for a startup MVP" [code screenshot]

### Pillar 2: EDUCATIONAL / VALUE (30% of posts)
Teach something. Builds authority and trust.

**Examples:**
- "5 reasons your startup doesn't need a mobile app yet (build a PWA instead)"
- "The exact stack I use for every MVP build and why"
- "Stop paying $5k/month for a no-code tool. Here's what a custom build actually costs"
- "How I scope a project in 24 hours (thread)"
- "3 mistakes founders make when hiring their first developer"

### Pillar 3: SOCIAL PROOF / RESULTS (20% of posts)
Show outcomes. This is what closes deals.

**Examples:**
- Client testimonials (even screenshots of WhatsApp/email praise)
- "Just delivered project #X. [Client name] went from idea to live product in 4 weeks."
- Revenue milestones: "Crossed $X in projects shipped this quarter"
- "Here's what my last 5 clients had in common" (thread about your ICP)

### Pillar 4: PERSONAL / FOUNDER STORY (10% of posts)
Be human. People buy from people.

**Examples:**
- "I left [previous situation] to build software for startups. Here's why."
- "What I learned from my first failed project"
- "A day in my life as a solo dev studio founder in Morocco"
- Hot takes: "Agencies are broken. Here's how solo studios win."

## 6. Paid Ads — Where, How Much, What to Target

### Phase 1: Start with Meta Ads (Facebook + Instagram) — Month 2

Meta has the best targeting for your budget. Don't start ads until you have at least 10-15 organic posts live (social proof on your profile).

#### Campaign 1: Landing Page Retargeting
- **Objective:** Conversions (contact form or WhatsApp click)
- **Audience:** Website visitors (install Meta Pixel on azinag.site first)
- **Budget:** $5/day ($150/month)
- **Ad format:** Single image or carousel showing your best work
- **Copy:** "Need a web app built fast? Fixed price, 2-6 week delivery. Book a free call →"
- **CTA button:** "Book Now" → link to azinag.site/contact or WhatsApp

#### Campaign 2: Cold Audience — Startup Founders
- **Objective:** Traffic → Landing page
- **Audience targeting:**
  - **Interests:** Startup, SaaS, Web development, Entrepreneurship, Y Combinator, Product Hunt, MVP
  - **Job titles:** Founder, CEO, CTO, Co-founder, Product Manager
  - **Age:** 24–45
  - **Locations:** United States, United Kingdom, Canada, UAE, France, Germany, Netherlands
  - **Exclude:** People who already visited your site
- **Budget:** $10/day ($300/month)
- **Ad format:** Video (screen recording of a project you built, 30-60 sec) or carousel
- **Landing page:** azinag.site/lp/startup-mvp-development (you already have this!)

#### Campaign 3: LinkedIn Ads (Month 3+, only if budget allows)
- LinkedIn ads are expensive ($8-15 per click) — only use when you have proven your offer converts
- **Format:** Sponsored post (boost your best-performing organic post)
- **Targeting:** Job title = Founder/CEO/CTO, Company size = 1-50, Industry = Technology
- **Budget:** $15/day ($450/month) — only if ROI from Meta is positive first

### Ad Creative Tips
- **Always show your work** — screenshots of real projects convert 3x better than stock photos
- **Use your face** — ads with a real person get 2x more clicks
- **Short copy wins** — Lead with the pain point: "Tired of agencies that ghost you?"
- **Test 3 variations** — change the headline, keep everything else the same. Kill losers after 3 days.

## 8. Funnels — How a Stranger Becomes a Client

```
AWARENESS               INTEREST                DECISION              ACTION
──────────             ──────────              ──────────            ──────────
Twitter/LinkedIn  →    Visit azinag.site  →    Read pricing    →    Book a call
post or ad             /showcase               /case study          or WhatsApp
                       (Meta Pixel fires)      (retargeting ad)     message
                                               
                  →    Landing page       →    Contact form    →    Discovery call
                       /lp/startup-mvp         submitted            → Proposal
                                                                    → Payment
```

### Key conversion points to optimize:
1. **Profile → Website click:** Make your bio/headline irresistible
2. **Website → Contact:** Strong CTAs, social proof, clear pricing
3. **Contact → Call:** Respond within 1 hour (WhatsApp helps here)
4. **Call → Paid project:** Have a clear proposal template ready

### Response time matters:
- Reply to DMs within **1 hour** during business hours
- Reply to contact form submissions within **2 hours**
- Send proposals within **24 hours** of discovery call
- **Speed = trust** for early-stage founders

## Important Mindset Rules

1. **Consistency > Perfection.** A mediocre post published beats a perfect post sitting in your drafts.
2. **Sell the outcome, not the tech.** Nobody cares about Next.js. They care about "launched in 3 weeks."
3. **DMs close deals, not posts.** Posts get attention. DMs convert. Always move conversations to DMs or calls.
4. **One client = referrals.** Every happy client should refer you to 2 more. Ask for it explicitly.
5. **Your portfolio is your best ad.** Every project you ship is marketing content. Document everything.
6. **Don't compete on price.** Compete on speed, reliability, and the founder-led experience. If someone wants cheap, let them go to Fiverr.
7. **Track everything.** If you can't measure it, you can't improve it.
